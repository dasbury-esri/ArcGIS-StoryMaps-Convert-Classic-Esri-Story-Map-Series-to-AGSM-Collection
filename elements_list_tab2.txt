<p><span style="font-size:20px"><span style="color:#E5FA84"><strong>10,317 buildings were tagged for demolition by the city of New Orleans.</strong></span></span></p>,
<p><span style="font-size:12px">Following Hurricane Katrina, all properties within the city were <em><a href="http://admarble.com/documents/winter07-08_000.pdf" target="_blank"><span style="color:#cccccc">reviewed for damage under Section 106</span></a></em> of the National Historic Preservation Act. This heat map shows the density of houses that were deemed eligible for federally-funded <em>demolition </em>through FEMA. Although not all properties on this map were demolished, the points illustrate Katrina's extensive and pervasive physical toll on the city of New Orleans. </span></p>,
